Kyle Hyer

Software Developer

I was born and raised in Nampa, Idaho and, though I've left it for one thing or another, still call it home. I attended Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah and got my B.S. in Histoy.

I attempted to persue my dream of becoming a professional ballroom dancer in Houston, Texas. Unfortunatly, trying to enter that world requires putting off having a family; something I was unwilling to do. I enjoyed the attempt, but eventually realized it did not align with my other goals in life.

I've been married to my wife since 2009 and since then we have brought three children into our family. We enjoy raising our children and teaching them how to care for chickens!

In my down time I enjoy reading, watching movies, and playing and designing table top games. I also love the outdoors and take pleasure in camping, fishing, canoeing, and skiing.

Coding background and Qualifications

I started coding in April 2017. Before that, I'd never even dabbled in it. One of my family members is also a software programmer and told me he thought I had what it would take to code. It also seems that everything around us now days requires some sort of coding. I've had plenty of jobs that were physically taxing and decided it was time to flex my brain instead. I do find it challenging, but whether I need to learn new frameworks or new languages, I'm up for it.
